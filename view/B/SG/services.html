Services

Fast and Reliable Delivery. We offer quick and reliable delivery services for all your furniture purchases. Our team ensures that your items arrive safely and on time. You can track your order through our website and contact our customer service for any assistance.

Delivery Costs: Delivery within Singapore typically costs around SGD 30 to SGD 60 depending on the size and weight of the furniture. For purchases over SGD 500, we offer free delivery.

Delivery Times: Most orders are delivered within 3 to 5 business days. For expedited delivery, you can opt for our express service which delivers within 1 to 2 business days at an additional cost.

Professional Assembly Services. We provide professional assembly services to make setting up your new furniture hassle-free. Our experts will handle everything from unpacking to assembling and placing your furniture exactly where you want it.

Assembly Costs: Assembly services are available for an additional fee of SGD 50 to SGD 150 depending on the complexity of the furniture. For items that require extensive assembly, we provide detailed cost estimates upfront.

Book an Assembly: You can easily book an assembly service during your checkout process. Simply select the assembly option, and our team will coordinate the best time to assemble your new furniture.

Comprehensive Building Guides. We understand that assembling furniture can be challenging. That's why we provide detailed building guides to make the process as smooth as possible. Each guide includes step-by-step instructions and clear illustrations.

Interactive Guides: Our interactive guides offer an innovative way to assemble your furniture. These guides are accessible via our website and provide a dynamic, user-friendly experience, ensuring you have all the help you need at every step.

Live Support: If you encounter any issues or have questions during the assembly process, our live support team is here to help. You can chat with our experts in real-time via our website for immediate assistance.
